SenseAgri

Agriculture, with its allied sectors, is unquestionably the largest livelihood provider in India, more so in the vast rural areas. It also contributes a significant figure to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP). Sustainable agriculture, in terms of food security, rural employment, and environmentally sustainable technologies such as soil conservation, sustainable natural resource management and biodiversity protection, are essential for holistic rural development. Indian agriculture and allied activities have witnessed a green revolution, a white revolution, a yellow revolution and a blue revolution.

This website provides information on agriculture Products Prices on real-time basis and it simultaneously draw up the detailed analysis using visualizations such as bar graphs , pie-charts etc. We have integrated our self-engineered Machine Learning Engine that provides the prediction of future prices of commodities.

It takes in various parameters and perform heavy computations to find the future predictions The accuracy is staggering 80% and will be improved overtime as the data grows in size



Predictive price analysis

It is always easy to visualize the data through graphs, pie-charts etc. We analyzed this need and created our indigenous visualization engine which helps the farmers, researchers to easily read the data and to draw some logical conclusion from the required data. As we all know that the era of Artificial Intelligence is upon us and every field is now employing some kind of Machine Learning. We realized this technology in agriculture and created our very own AI algorithm which predicts the future prices of the commodities fairly accurately and improves with time.


map wise price distribution

Often we find farmers in a dilemma over the pricing issue. We identified the issue and designed our system to provide data of the nearby markets displays interactively on maps
Map is an easy mode of communication which convey the information in an interactive fashion and they are now being used in every sector of industry. So our system automatically detects the location of the user , it then find out all the nearby markets in a 50 mile radius and draw up the real-time prices data on the map so the user can get a Â sense of what is happening in the nearby markets . We also have a custom search feature wherein the user enter some location and find the price of commodity in the nearby markets.
